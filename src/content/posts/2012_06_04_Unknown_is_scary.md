Not knowing something might mean nothing

but it is when you really want to know it.

The scariest question of all time, what is there waiting in front of me.

Rather than worried about them

perhaps we should embrace the future

prepare yourself, so that whatever showing up there

you are able to welcome it with your hand wide open for a hug

for it is your future, and yours alone
