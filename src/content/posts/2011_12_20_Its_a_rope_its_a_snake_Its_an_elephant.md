Whatever you do , there is no way to prove that you are right.

You might think right is not wrong , so if you are not doing anything wrong , you must be right

but this is not the case , consider doing nothing ... its nothing wrong with it , but it certainly not right either

People always looking for answer , truth , path to ever happiness .

Problem is , they are just simply myth.

Whoever says that they have to answer to everything are probably liar or troll.

Whatever right to him might not suite you.

Bird fly , fish swim and human walk .. we all have our own way to live and own set of right and wrong.

If everyone have their own set of right and wrong , why we have to bother whatever we done are right or wrong ?

because we human are social animal, our sense of right and wrong are largely depending on the acknowledgement from the other people.

Thats why sometime we feel bad when people judging us.

We hate the feeling so we begin to lie to ourself that we shouldn't give a shit about what people think about us.

That is so not true, we care about what people think of us, the reason the lie are not going away is because there is enough people to support us that we can ignore anyone who judging us.

If its 1 people , we can just safely ignore him

If its 2 peoples , it might feel not right , but nonetheless can live with it.

If its 3 , 4 ,5 , 6  people, can you still think that you don't give a fuck of what people think of you ? unless you are a superstar , where you have 50000 people hating you and another 50000 people loving you.

If we live for ourself , and our sense of right and wrong coming from the crowd , there must be something wrong somewhere.

2 of them cannot coexist with each other.

In order for them to coexist , you need to acknowledge that you are not always right , your way of living are wrong.

You might want to correct it and accept the fact that you are wrong and its OK for you to live with it.

You never know if you are doing it wrong or right.

Because everyone is different.

if you can rely on the crowd , you can only count on yourself.

because your sense of right and wrong come from the crowd.
